[color=purple]          [b]► Character related[/b][/color]
[color=pink]                    [b]!register[/b][/color]: Register to the database.
[color=pink]                    [b]!card[/b][/color]: Prints your Character Card in public.
[color=pink]                    [b]!cardcode[/b][/color]: Prints the code so you can copy and paste it to your profile.
[color=pink]                    [b]!sheet[/b][/color]: Prints your Character Sheet (the complete info) in a private message.
[color=pink]                    [b]!sheetcode[/b][/color]: Prints the code so you can copy and paste it to your profile.
[color=pink]                    [b]!sheetcolors[/b][/color]: Sets custom colors for your card and sheet. Example: [b]!sheetcolors green and yellow[/b]
[color=pink]                    [b]!train {ATK/DEF} on {STAT}[/b][/color]: Increases by 1 the selected stat. Costs $10.00 and max 5 on a stat without items.
[color=pink]                    [b]!indulge {ATK/DEF} on {STAT}[/b][/color]: Decreases by 1 the selected stat. You get $10.00 back and max -3 on a stat without items.
                    Options: attack/defense to lips/fingers/tits/sex/ass/feet. Example: [b]!train defense on ass[/b]
[color=pink]                    [b]!sendmoney # to {PERSON}[/b][/color]: Sends # money to the specified person. Example: [b]!sendmoney 1 to Kenia Nya[/b]
[color=pink]                    [b]!resetcharacter[/b][/color]: Deletes all your items and stats but gives you the starting money, leaving you like if you just registered.

[color=pink]                    [b]!sell {ITEM}[/b][/color]: Sells the item named {ITEM}, and gives you half the original cost back.
[color=pink]                    [b]!equip {ITEM}[/b][/color]: Equips the item named {ITEM}, if you have it in your inventory.
